About us

Ionic was founded in 2012, when using web technologies as a means to build native apps was still in its infancy. When we started, we just wanted to create a better way for web developers to use their existing skillsets to build apps for the app stores.

Today, Ionic is the world's most popular cross-platform mobile development technology stack, powering fast growing startups to some of the biggest companies in the world.

Millions of developers from nearly every industry around the world have built millions of mobile apps with Ionic. Our platform allows developers to use web technologies to build and deliver high quality apps for mobile and desktop, with everything they need from front-end design to continuous delivery.

Ionic not only makes app development easier, faster, and more accessible—it makes it more fun. Mobile is all about finding new ways to change the way humans interact with the world, and we couldn’t think of anything more fun than that.

Our Mission

Ionic’s mission is to provide open source tools and services that empower people around the world to participate in the fast-growing mobile app economy. Read about our values.

Our Philosophy

We always bet on the web. It has served developers well for the last twenty years, and we think that makes it a pretty great bet for the next twenty. Get to know our team.

Our neck of the woods

Ionic was proudly founded and headquartered in beautiful Madison, Wisconsin, USA. Located on an isthmus between two lakes, we love being able to walk or bike to our office, located in the heart of the downtown State Capital.

Although many of us work (and play) in Madison, we also have satellite offices in Milwaukee and Boston and a large group of remote workers scattered around the United States and Europe.
